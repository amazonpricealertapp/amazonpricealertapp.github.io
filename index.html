Amazon Price Alert


The new app that helps you save money on Amazon!

Features

Search

Search on Amazon to find the products you are interested in and add them to the list of the items to be monitored.

Price notifications

Receive a notification when the prices of your favorite products change. You can set a threashold price for every item you are monitoring.

In-app shop

You don't have to leave the app. Just buy from the Amazon page and get the best price on the market!

Barcode scan

Do you have a physical product and want to know the price on Amazon or directly buy it? You can simply use the integrated Barcode scanner!
